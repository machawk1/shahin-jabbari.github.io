I am a CRCS postdoctoral fellow in the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Harvard hosted by Milind Tambe. I recently completed my PhD in the Computer and Information Science Department at University of Pennsylvania where I was fortunate to be advised by Michael Kearns.

Research

I study the interactions between machine learning and a variety of contexts, ranging from crowdsourcing to game theory, AI for social good and algorithmic fairness.
